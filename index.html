What is CallUp ?                                                      

CallUp is a social network application that allows its users to interact in a way that suits their preferences. Upon creating their account, each user can select the profile(s) they want and easily navigate through them. These profiles are designed to offer an environment tailored to each user. For instance, someone who wants to simply have fun with friends can select the social profile, gaining access to an environment that allows for messaging, joining video calls, and even enjoying mini-games. Additionally, users can create groups of up to 10 people, with an unlimited group size for premium users, making it easier to share and enjoy activities solo or with others.
